Profit Calculation Rules

Cumulative Calculation

Profit is calculated as total percentage gain from starting balance over the full 2-month period, not monthly averages.

Distribution Flexibility

You can achieve the 6% target in any distribution. Example: +8% Month 1, -1% Month 2 = 7% total = PASS.

No Monthly Minimum

There is no minimum profit requirement per month. Only the cumulative 2-month total matters.
