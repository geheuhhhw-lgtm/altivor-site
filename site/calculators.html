RR Calculator

Risk-Reward Ratio

Enter your entry price, stop loss, and take profit target. The calculator returns the RR ratio and evaluates the setup quality.

Result
Risk (points)
Reward (points)
Min. win rate needed
Formula: RR = |Entry - Take Profit| |Entry - Stop Loss|. A ratio of 1:1 is breakeven at 50% win rate. Minimum recommended: 1:2.

Profit / Loss

Calculates the realised profit or loss based on entry and exit prices, lot size, and instrument contract value.

Result
Price movement
Value per point
Formula: P/L = (Exit - Entry) Lots Value per point. For short positions the sign is reversed.

Per night
