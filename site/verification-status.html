Challenge Rule Framework

The US100 Validation Challenge is evaluated on procedural compliance, not financial outcome alone. Before activation, you must acknowledge and accept the following rule framework.

Session Window Trades may only be opened within the permitted challenge execution window.
Maximum Risk Risk per trade must not exceed the defined maximum allocation threshold.
Stop Loss Requirement Every trade must have a stop loss defined at entry. Trades without stop loss are treated as hard breaches.
Trade Documentation Each trade must include valid documentation (entry, exit, screenshot, notes). Incomplete trades may be invalidated.
Framework Compliance Each trade must comply with the defined execution framework. Non-framework trades may be invalidated or treated as breaches.
Invalidation vs Disqualification Some violations invalidate a single trade (it does not count). Others disqualify the entire challenge immediately and permanently.

Challenge Rule Matrix

Complete rule framework for the US100 Validation Challenge. All rules are fixed and deterministic.

Hard Fail — Challenge Disqualification

Any single occurrence results in immediate and permanent challenge failure.

  • Trade opened outside the permitted challenge session window
  • Risk per trade exceeds the maximum allowed threshold
  • No stop loss defined at trade entry
  • Manual widening of stop loss beyond original structural invalidation level
  • Trade documentation intentionally missing where submission is required
  • Maximum challenge drawdown (10%) breached
  • Broker statement requirement not fulfilled by final verification stage
  • Any explicitly prohibited execution behavior defined by the challenge framework

Invalid Trade Rules

Trade is excluded from validated count but does not disqualify the challenge.

  • Trade not assigned to a recognized strategy or framework type
  • Required structure confirmation missing at entry
  • Entry taken without required pullback condition (where applicable)
  • Incomplete trade log, missing screenshots, or missing required metadata
  • Missing required validation fields (entry, exit, stop loss, take profit)
  • Trade cannot be mapped to a valid execution setup under challenge rules

Warning Rules

Affect discipline, consistency, or trader score. Do not directly invalidate or disqualify.

  • Overtrading pattern detected (excessive trades per session)
  • Revenge-trade timing pattern (post-loss impulsive re-entry)
  • Repeated inconsistency between declared and executed risk
  • Repeated non-fatal checklist deviations
  • Quality degradation patterns across multiple trades

What Counts as a Valid Trade

  • Trade opened within the permitted session window
  • Risk allocation within maximum threshold
  • Stop loss defined at entry
  • Complete trade documentation (entry, exit, SL, TP, screenshots, notes)
  • Trade mapped to a recognized framework setup
  • No active hard-fail or invalidation rule triggered

Required Evidence and Documentation

  • Trade entry and exit prices with timestamps
  • Stop loss and take profit levels
  • Trade screenshot (chart or platform confirmation)
  • Trade notes describing setup rationale
  • Broker statement (required once, before final verification)
  • Weekly equity check-ins (8 required across the evaluation period)
