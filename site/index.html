The Institute

Built as a structural
execution institute.

Market participants operate in environments defined more by behavioral inconsistency than by genuine structural edge. ALTIVOR INSTITUTE was established to address this directly — by providing operational frameworks that convert subjective trading decisions into auditable, measurable process data.

The objective is not prediction. It is structural discipline. Each framework within ALTIVOR defines clear execution conditions, risk rules, and documentation protocols to separate process-consistent behavior from reactive noise.

01

Decision Architecture

Each framework specifies the precise conditions under which an execution decision is considered valid. Deviations are documented and reviewed as part of the validation cycle.

02

Risk Governance

Fixed drawdown thresholds, position sizing constraints, and daily exposure limits are embedded structurally — not applied situationally or adjusted post-hoc.

03

Validation Systems

The 55 trade Cycle is a strategy verification system. Across 55 documented trades, it determines whether your strategy holds under structured conditions — exposing consistency, discipline, execution quality and behavioral patterns.

Operational Infrastructure

The Altivor Operational Panel

The ALTIVOR Operational Panel is the participant's primary interface during an active validation cycle. It is a structured logging and compliance environment — not a performance dashboard. The panel captures process data, not financial results.

Trade Log

A structured, session-by-session trade entry interface. Participants document each trade including instrument, direction, entry and exit points, size, and protocol compliance notes.

55 trade Cycle Counter

Real-time tracking of the 55 trade Cycle — a strategy verification system that shows whether your strategy performs consistently. Measures rule adherence, behavioral control and execution quality across the full 55-trade sequence.

Risk Governance Module

A live display of current drawdown position relative to defined maximum thresholds. Includes daily exposure utilization and position-level risk allocation monitoring.

Behavioral Audit

A structured review module that flags protocol deviations, records decision rationale, and maintains a complete chronological audit record across the full validation cycle.

Monthly Report Export

At the end of each calendar month, participants may export a structured PDF behavioral audit report covering all trade activity, compliance metrics, and deviation events within that period.

Execution Compliance Score

A per-rule breakdown of compliance across the active validation cycle. Each of the six protocol rules is tracked independently, exposing which specific conditions are generating non-compliant outcomes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ALTIVOR INSTITUTE?
ALTIVOR INSTITUTE is a structural execution institute that develops operational frameworks for systematic market participation. It does not provide signals, advisory services, investment management, or any form of performance guarantee.
What is the US100 framework?
The US100 Framework is ALTIVOR's active process-validation product for Nasdaq 100 CFD. It combines strict execution rules, fixed risk governance, full documentation standards and a 55 trade Cycle — a strategy verification system designed to determine whether your strategy works, not to measure profit.
What is the 55 trade Cycle?
The 55 trade Cycle is the framework's strategy verification system. It is not a challenge and it is not profit-based. Across 55 documented trades, the system determines whether your trading strategy actually works under disciplined conditions — measuring rule adherence, risk discipline, execution quality and behavioral stability to reveal if the trader follows a system or reacts emotionally.
What is the access structure?
Access at ALTIVOR INSTITUTE is structured in three layers. First, PREPARE (29 —) — a mandatory one-time procedural qualification gate. Second, a Challenge product (e.g. US100 at 129 —). Third, the Accessories suite — a separate monthly subscription at 79 € / month, discounted to 59 € / month for participants who have successfully passed a challenge.
Is this a funded trading programme?
No. ALTIVOR INSTITUTE does not provide capital, funded accounts, or any form of financial allocation. It provides structural documentation and operational tooling for participants who wish to validate their own execution process.
What are the subscription fees for Accessories?
The Accessories suite is available via a monthly subscription at 79 € / month. Participants who successfully pass a Challenge are permanently eligible for a discounted rate of 59 € / month.
What broker or platform do I need?
The US100 framework is designed for execution via MetaTrader 5 on a CFD instrument linked to the Nasdaq 100. Structural analysis is conducted via TradingView. ALTIVOR INSTITUTE does not recommend, endorse, or partner with any specific brokerage.
Does ALTIVOR provide trading signals or advice?
No. ALTIVOR INSTITUTE does not provide market signals, trade recommendations, investment advice, or any form of directional market guidance. All market decisions remain the sole responsibility of the participant.
How is payment processed?
Payment is processed via Stripe. All transactions are secured via industry-standard encryption. ALTIVOR INSTITUTE does not store card details. Upon successful payment confirmation, an account is created and access is provisioned automatically.
Can I upgrade from Framework Pack to Full Access?
Yes. Participants holding Framework Pack access may upgrade to Full Access at any time by completing an additional payment equivalent to the difference in plan pricing.
