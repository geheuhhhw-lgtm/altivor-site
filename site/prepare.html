Procedural Qualification Gate

PREPARE Procedural Qualification System

Mandatory procedural gate validating structural readiness before Challenge access. Complete 10 compliant trades to qualify (100% compliance threshold).

Mandatory Prior to Challenge Access
29 EUR
Section 01

Purpose & Scope

PREPARE is not a performance benchmark. It is a procedural gate a structural validation mechanism that confirms a participant's ability to execute a declared trading process without deviation, prior to gaining access to the US100 Challenge.

Mandate: Completion of PREPARE is a prerequisite. No participant may access the US100 Challenge without first demonstrating procedural compliance through this qualification stage.
Strategy Requirement: Participants must have their own developed Core Strategy and Sniper Strategy. Upon purchasing the US100 Challenge, you receive a 55 trade Cycle that validates whether your strategies work under disciplined conditions.
Section 02

Qualification Requirements

To pass PREPARE, you must submit 10 compliant trades out of 10 (100% compliance threshold). Each trade is evaluated as PASS or FAIL based on structural rule compliance financial outcome carries zero evaluative weight.

  • Instrument must be US100 (NASDAQ-100 Index CFD) or approved alias
  • Stop Loss must be set at entry (mandatory)
  • Each trade must be a completed position (entry and exit recorded)
  • Maximum 10 lot position size per trade
  • TradingView chart screenshot required for each submission
  • Submitted data must match your broker records exactly
Section 03

Disqualification & Cooldown

If you accumulate 20 non-compliant submissions, PREPARE access is temporarily suspended with a mandatory 7-day cooldown period.

This mechanism exists to prevent reactive execution patterns following consecutive procedural failures. Access is automatically restored after the cooldown expires.

Section 04

Structural Exclusions

The following are explicitly excluded from the PREPARE qualification framework:

  • Performance targets or profit requirements
  • Win rate thresholds
  • Time-based completion pressure
  • Drawdown limits
  • Account balance requirements
  • Minimum trade frequency
Section 05

What's Included After Purchase

Upon completing your PREPARE purchase, you gain immediate access to the following components:

Qualification System

Full access to the interactive PREPARE qualification interface with real-time compliance tracking and trade submission.

Challenge Unlock

Successful PREPARE qualification unlocks access to purchase Framework Pack and US100 Challenge products.

All components are delivered immediately upon purchase. PREPARE is a one-time qualification no recurring fees.
